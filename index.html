Welcome to Lake Huyck Resort

This is not a cookie-cutter, generic Airbnb experience. Our homes have been meticulously designed and curated with original décor and amenities.

Nestled on the shores of a private glacial spring-fed lake, our resort offers two distinct cabins—each with its own character, but sharing the same breathtaking setting. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway or a family reunion, Lake Huyck Resort delivers an unforgettable lakefront experience.
