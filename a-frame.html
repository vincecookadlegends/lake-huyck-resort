A Vintage, Rustic, Enchanting Escape

Welcome to our freshly and fully remodeled A-frame cabin, loaded with thoughtful details and nestled inside a woodsy backdrop on the shores of a private glacial lake with a sandy, clear beach and wonderful docks for swimming, paddle boarding, canoeing, and playing.

Built with a stunning cathedral living room featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and skylights, spilling in sunshine and opening to beautiful lake views. The newly designed full kitchen and dining space flows seamlessly into the living area, while the tiered deck offers even more spectacular lake views—perfect for enjoying meals or simply relaxing.

Enjoy beautiful summer days, fall leaves in full blaze, a gazillion stars, s'mores and campfire stories, boating, snow skiing, fishing, beaver and bald eagle watching, swimming, hiking, reading on docks and decks, catching sunrises, sunshine and sunsets—and let clouds and time pass by with family and friends.

Cabin Highlights

  • Cathedral ceilings with floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Skylights flooding the space with natural light
  • Newly remodeled spa-like bathroom
  • Blue-green herringbone tiles inspired by the lake
  • Custom reclaimed wood vanity with porcelain bowl
  • Rain shower with vaulted spa-like ceilings
  • Tiered deck with panoramic lake views
  • Pendleton throws and quality bedding throughout
