Local Guide

Our favorite restaurants, breweries, wineries, and adventures near the resort.
Everything is within a short scenic drive from your cabin.

Dining

From lakeside supper clubs to farm-to-table fine dining, southwest Michigan has an incredible food scene. Here are the spots we keep coming back to.

The Rock At Big Fish Lake

6 min away

Great supper club with very good steaks, bluegill tacos, cocktails and sunset views. Taco and pizza bar patio overlook the lake. It gets crowded but worth the wait.

Amy's Fabulous Diner II

6 min away · Marcellus

Great local cafe. Fantastic breakfasts and lunches. The Benedicts are super — Cubano or Brisket. Giant cups of coffee.

Longship Brewing Company

9 mi away · Lawton

Live folk and blues music on some nights and weekends. Fantastic brews and delicious menu. Very cool and rustic, warm joint. Best burger we've had in five years. Fish tacos and scratch made desserts are fantastic.

Bird Dog

Mattawan

Make reservations ahead — this is a good one. Wild game, some great food and probably an unforgettable meal.

Journeyman Distillery

Three Oaks

Great distillery with a full restaurant, 18 hole mini golf, corn hole, and much more. Fantastic cocktails and menu. Great place to pick up some locally made whiskey and swag on your way in.

Craftsman Chop Co

Kalamazoo

One of the finest chop houses in the area. Fantastic steaks, selection of wines and a cozy setting.

BOLD

Kalamazoo

For a nice night out and a little bit fancier, Bold has a wonderful menu.

Oak and Ash BBQ & Catering

15 min drive · Lawton

Giant family restaurant, very charming, huge beer list and something for everyone.

La Cantina

Paw Paw

80+ year old restaurant with classic Italian food and a great patio by the river.

Amore Pizza

Paw Paw

Great pizza. Probably the best in 50 miles.

Strand Brewery & Kitchen

15 min drive · Sister Lakes

Great brewery on the lake with live music Friday and Saturday.

Hank's Tavern on the River

15 min drive

Great spot for beer, cocktails, lunch and dinner.

Dockside | Dining & Drinks

Sister Lakes

Pizza next to a lake, music and sunsets. Perfection.

The Joint at Rainbow Farms

6 min away · Marcellus

A quirky restaurant with a good sub menu and ice cream bar.

The Vineyard Café

9 mi away · Lawton

Cute, simple, little diner. A true local hang out. Great food and breakfasts.

Oaks Eatery

Three Oaks

Small cafe — maybe a stop on your way in from Chicago in the morning. Fantastic menus and food.

Copper Ridge Inn & Market

Nearby

Call ahead for schedule. Fresh farm breakfasts.

Sly's Seafood & Co.

Nearby

Fresh seafood spot worth checking out.

Diamond Dough & Co.

Cassopolis

Best donuts in south Michigan. Just look at the pictures.

Scott's Pig Roast

Nearby

Local favorite for smoked meats and BBQ.

Outdoors & Parks

From internationally designated dark sky parks to creekside forest trails, the natural beauty around Lake Huyck is something special.

Dr. Lawless International Dark Sky Park

Nearby

One of just a few designated Dark Parks in the US for star watching. Also a great place for picnics, hiking and playgrounds.

Meyer Broadway County Park

Nearby

Great for almost any activity. Beautiful hiking, disc golf, sledding.

Fred Russ Forest Park

Nearby

Fantastic hike for family or dog with beautiful creekside paths.

Star Gazing Tip

Dr. Lawless Dark Sky Park is one of the best places in the Midwest to see the Milky Way. Best viewing on clear, moonless nights. Bring a blanket and give your eyes 20 minutes to adjust.
