Rustic Living

We are experienced new owners whose intent is sharing our homes to make wonderful stays. We embrace rustic living and the adventure it brings. Nature surrounds the resort and those that are into nature and all its beauty and charm will absolutely love the experience. Those that see nature as a nuisance might not appreciate it.

Country Sounds

It's rustic and we'd never change a thing. It's also COUNTRY out here so please keep in mind you may hear some unusual sounds not heard in the city or burbs. Although we would like to control nature for you we can't. The occasional freight train may pass by and blow his whistle or the crickets seem to be louder out here. You'll hear wildlife: chipmunks fussing, owls hooting, bullfrogs croaking, birds you've never heard before singing and that may annoy some because it's too early or all night. It comes with its own squeaky floors and earthy tasting water but pure.

Nature's Guests

There's bugs, spiders, or gnats around lights or mosquitos that you may have to slap. That's just nature saying hello. Mice, critters or chipmunks love to try and sneak in when no one's looking so if you see any creepy crawly in there, there's no need for alarm. Just show him the way outside.
