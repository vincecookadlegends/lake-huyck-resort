Vintage, Rustic, Enchanting

Welcome to our freshly and fully remodeled, vintage, rustic, enchanting lake log cabin loaded with thoughtful details. Nestled inside a woodsy backdrop on the shores of a private glacial lake with a sandy, clear beach and wonderful docks for swimming, paddle boarding, canoeing, and playing.

The living space features a lush, lake backdrop with 2 large leather couches, throw blankets, a rocking chair, games, and a large tree stump table. It opens into a newly remodeled chef's kitchen with open shelves, butcher block counters, deep double-sink, and retro style refrigerator—made for preparing gourmet, Michigan-inspired meals.

The dining and living space are perfect for morning coffee, crackling fires, evening drinks and conversations with friends, watching the kids play, taking in a good book, nature, or just doing nothing at all.

Master Suite

Guest Bedroom

Bunk Room

Guest Bathroom
